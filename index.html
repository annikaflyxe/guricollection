GURI SISTERS

We are Josi and Delhi Premoli, The Guri sisters and the soul of Guri Collection. We are two Argentinian sisters who have grown up in cities all over the world, but now lives in Barcelona and Copenhagen.

We are both passionate about living a beautiful life, exploring the world, and doing good - and this is what we want to achieve with our brand.

We invented this company to allow other like minded women to always look put together while they’re on the go, no matter what the activity or weather is. We believe that there shouldn’t be a compromise between looking effortlessly good, being fashion-forward, and living consciously.

Our hairclaws are a fun and colorful accessory to elevate any look. Pair them poolside, for a chic night out, or out and about in the city for a fun, effortless, yet put together look.
